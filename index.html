A Home Away from Home

Play Buddies opened its doors in 2008 with a simple belief: every child deserves to feel safe, loved and free to explore. We understand how precious children are and never take for granted the tremendous privilege of sharing our lives with them.

We are grateful that families choose to put their trust in us to care for their little angels. Our focus is on developing friendships through play and promoting emotional, social, intellectual and physical development.

We can't promise we'll never lose a sock or send home the wrong juice bottle — but we can promise the children in our care will never be short a cuddle and will always be loved.

What We Offer

Growing Through Play

An informal approach that creates happy, self-confident children ready for nursery school.

🧸

Small Group Setting

With fewer than 30 children, every child receives the individual attention and care they deserve.

🎨

Creative Expression

Daily arts, crafts and messy play encourage creativity and self-expression in a fun environment.

🎵

Music & Movement

Singing, dancing and rhythm activities develop coordination and build confidence every day.

🌿

Outdoor Adventures

Sand play, water play, bikes and gross motor activities keep little bodies active and engaged.

📚

School Readiness

Basic tools for nursery school — shapes, colours, numbers and social skills — all through play.

🏡

Homely Environment

A safe, secure and stimulating space where children feel loved and families feel at ease.
